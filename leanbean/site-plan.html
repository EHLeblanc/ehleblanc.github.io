Content

Home page

It will introduce the company and brand. Why they should my jellybeans vs the competitors. That our jellybeans are a healthy alternative to the jellybeans laden with sugar from most of our competitors. With less amount of sugar in our jellybeans, you will feel less guilty eating them.

Images for the Home page

Flavours

It will list the flavours we have. Talk about all the combination of flavors we have. We have some of the tried and true fruit and berry falors. We also have some exotic flavors to appease those people who have a flair for more adventure. We also can do custom jellybeans for a special occasion like a wedding, or birth of a child.

Images for the Page 2

About us

It will tell how the company started and a little bit about the process on making the sugar-free jelly beans. How we started out from humble beginnings. Just a couple of people who liked eating jellybeans, but did not want to feel so guilty from eating something with so sugar. We knew that there had to be more people like us, feeling same way. That is why we came up with a healhier altenative.
